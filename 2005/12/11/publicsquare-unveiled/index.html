PublicSquare Unveiled

December 11, 2005 · 77 words · 1 min read

Lars and Christina finally reveal some information on their secret project #2. The name of the game is PublicSquare, a platform for citizen journalism, we media in the current jargon.

Knowing the skills of the people behind PublicSquare, I can’t wait to get my hands on the real thing. It’s already running behind Boxes and Arrows, so the golden rule of frameworks being extractions seems to be holding true. Needless to mention, PS is running on Rails.
